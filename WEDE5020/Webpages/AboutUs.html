About Us

History 🏠

Founded in 2024, Phiri's Whiskful Treats began as a family kitchen project supplying cupcakes and pastries. Today, we bake cakes, cookies, bread, and desserts for everyone!

Mission 🎯

To create moments of happiness by serving freshly baked goods made with love, creativity, and passion.

Vision 🌟

To become the most loved bakery in South Africa through great recipes, friendly service, and community spirit.

Who We Serve 💕

Families, couples, students, tourists, cafés, event planners, and businesses looking for sweet treats and catering.
